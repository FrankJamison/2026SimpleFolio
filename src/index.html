About me

I am a developer with a background in education, which means I do not just write code. I think about clarity, structure, and how systems make sense to other people. My move into software development was intentional. I have built real projects using Python, Flask, JavaScript, and SQL, with a focus on clean architecture, maintainability, and thoughtful design.

I enjoy building practical tools such as budgeting applications, RPG utilities, portfolio platforms, and small business websites. I care about performance, accessibility, and interfaces that feel purposeful instead of cluttered. I am especially drawn to projects where logic and creativity intersect, where engineering meets craftsmanship.

Outside of coding, I am a longtime stamp collector and student of visual design through historical print media. Studying stamps has sharpened my eye for margins, balance, typography, and detail. I also write about web development and technical thinking, exploring how structure and storytelling work together.

My goal is to continue growing as a full stack developer, contribute to meaningful software, and work with teams that value precision and steady improvement. I am focused on building durable skills and producing work that lasts.
